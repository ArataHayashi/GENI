WELCOME TO  GENI

~ Gene ENrichment Identifier ~

An open web tool for easy analysis of Gene Set Enrichment Analysis in clinical data.

GENI enables intuitive analysis of gene set enrichment in cancer patients' gene expression without prior software experience. It gives you inspiration on how the gene of your interest affects a set of genes based on the gene expression of cancer patients from clinical studies.

 WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH GENI ?:

  • Find the gene sets that are enriched with your gene of interest from cancer patient data(TCGA).
  • Apply Gene Set Enrichment Analysis (GSEA) easily on your rank file. 
  • Compare gene set enrichments between different patient data / genes (Upcoming).
